Immersive Language Clubs in your city!

Come and have a lot of fun in our Immersive Language Clubs! You will meet people from around the world, make new friends and practice your favorite language. With sessions available every weekend of august!

Our Method
Language Exchanges

Feel free to share your language with the rest of the participants, you never know who is eager to practice your mother language

Cultural Immersion

You will not only practice another language, but you will also get see other people's culture to know their language better

Grammar Focused

Rest assured that our participants will always correct your grammar mistakes in a polite and constructive manner while you keep up the conversation

Formal and casual speech

Language is not only grammar an books, learn how to express yourself in a casual manner as well

Fun and games!

In order to make it more fun for you and us there are a lot of conversational interactive games
